# Legal and Use Constraints

ArgusNet is provided as an open-source research and engineering platform for world modeling, localization, mapping, inspection, coverage planning, replay, and evaluation workflows.

## Use Boundary

This project is intended for lawful, authorized, and safety-bounded applications, including:

- aerial mapping
- infrastructure inspection
- construction and site documentation
- environmental survey
- synthetic data generation
- localization and navigation benchmarking
- controlled field testing and academic research

This project is **not** intended for:

- weapon systems or targeting support
- autonomous engagement
- unlawful surveillance
- cyber intrusion, takeover, or unauthorized control of vehicles or infrastructure
- deployments that violate aviation, privacy, export-control, or local operating laws

## User Responsibility

By using this repository, you are responsible for:

- ensuring you have the right to collect, process, and store any data involved in your workflow
- complying with all applicable laws, regulations, permits, licenses, and contractual restrictions
- following aviation, privacy, safety, and site-access requirements in the jurisdictions where you operate
- independently validating any real-world deployment before use

## No Operational Authorization

Nothing in this repository grants flight permission, surveillance authority, site access, or legal clearance. Documentation, examples, simulation artifacts, and source code are informational and technical resources, not legal advice or operational approval.
